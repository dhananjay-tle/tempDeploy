NASA End-User Services & Technologies (NEST)

As part of Team Leidos, Altech provides, manages, secures, and maintains essential IT services for 60,000 end users. We're proud to be NASA's agile, trusted partner in transforming, integrating, modernizing, and safeguarding NASA's IT systems and data. Above all, the NEST program focuses on continuously improving the end-user experience.

Advanced Enterprise Global Information Technology Solutions (AEGIS)

AEGIS is designed to provide seamless communication infrastructure, spanning both wide-area and center local-area networks. It supports critical systems like cybersecurity, collaboration tools, emergency warning systems, telephony, cabling, and radio systems. Altech, in partnership with Prime Leidos, has teams stationed at five NASA centers, ensuring secure and efficient operations.

United States Nuclear Regulatory Commission (USNRC)

Altech is a subcontractor on the NRC's Global Infrastructure and Development Acquisition (GLINDA) program, providing comprehensive IT support to NRC Headquarters in Rockville, MD, along with regional offices in King of Prussia, PA; Atlanta, GA; Lisle, IL; and Arlington, TX, as well as the NRC Technical Training Center in Chattanooga, TN. Under this flexible Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) - Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract, Altech delivers key IT services, including seat services, network services, and operational security services, ensuring smooth IT operations and enhancing the NRC's mission-critical capabilities.
